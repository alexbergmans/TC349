Music

I am a huge fan of music, and when I say that I mean that I like every genre there is. I listen to albums from the 1950's and I listen to albums that came out yesterday. It's a passion of mine and I'm constantly looking for and listening to albums and works that are interesting, intriguing, and just plain enjoyable.

On this page, I plan on posting reviews of certain albums, as well as top lists of my favorite artists, songs, albums, whatever. It's going to be a lot of fun for me and I hope you enjoy it.
