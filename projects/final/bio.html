Bio

Alex Bergmans is a student and tech enthusiast from Canton, Michigan. He studied Media and Information at Michigan State University, and is drawn to web design and administration, as he's always found webpages to be intriguing. He's passionate about film, music and programming. During his years at Michigan State, Alex worked at Brody Square where he worked as a Student Cook Supervisor for the on-campus culinary services. During his summers in college, he also worked at Salem Hills Golf Course as a groundscrew and maintenance worker.

In addition to working as a Student Cook, Alex also served as the Butterfield residence hall Treasurer during his years in the university dormitories. In his spare time, you'll find Alex playing guitar or watching and enjoying films, particularly the ouevre of Scorsese or Kubrick. He also is an avid music fans and has listened to every album on Roger Dimery's 1001 Albums You Must Hear Before You Die. Alex's personal slogan is "Everything will work out, so there's no use in stressing." Find Alex on Twitter at @alexbergmans.

Education

Alex spent his early education days in the Plymouth-Canton Community Schools public school system. He attended Bentley Elementary School from Kindergarten to 2nd Grade, and Miller Elementary School from 3rd to 5th Grade. He transferred to the other Elementary for 3rd grade because he was accepted into the school system's Talented and Gifted program. He attended middle school, or grades 6 through 8 at East Middle School in Plymouth, MI. For High School he attended the Plymouth-Canton Educational Park, which contains three schools: Plymouth High School, Salem High School, and Canton High School. He was officially a member of Plymouth High School, though he attended classes at all three. He was also a member of the National Honors Society during his High School years.

After graduating from Plymouth High School, Alex attended Michigan State University. He has attended the University since August 2010. He is enrolled in the Media and Information degree program
